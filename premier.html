About the Premier Shakers program
Do Premier Protein products play a significant role in your personal health and wellness journey? Do you enjoy being an active voice for your friends, family and local community? If this sounds like you, apply for our Premier Shaker program to get exclusive access to Premier Protein products + experiences and be the first to hear about exciting news + events! Each Premier Shaker joins a community of friends that will cheer you on and support your health journey. Plus, you will get to inspire and encourage others!

We're looking for Shakers to document their experience with Premier Protein and participate in our monthly post prompt. Help share the love through photos, recipes + inspiring content that encourages friends + family to try Premier Protein. Show others what all the hype is about!

Who are Premier Shakers?
You should become a Premier Shaker if you're:
  • Passionate about Premier Protein’s products, which play an important role in your health journey.
  • Loud on Social. For us, it’s quality, not quantity. Our Shakers don’t need massive social followings, just as long as you can spread the love to your engaged network.
  • Excited to celebrate all of life’s little wins and recognize progress over perfection.
  • An optimistic and encouraging voice in your community. We’re all about good energy, and finding ways to keep it flowing.
Premier Shaker Perks:
Early access to new products
  • Branded swag
  • Exclusive Recipe Content
  • A support network of Shakers all around the country
  • Opportunity to be featured in Premier Protein marketing
  • Gifts and surprises throughout the year!
